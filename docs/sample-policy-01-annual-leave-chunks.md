## 2. Accrual and Allowance
* **Full-Time Employees:** All standard full-time employees at ttb Policy Assistant Demo Bank accrue annual leave at a rate of 22 business days per calendar year. Accrual begins on the first calendar day of employment and is credited monthly.
* **Part-Time Employees:** Part-time employees accrue annual leave on a pro-rata basis based on their contracted weekly hours compared to a standard 40-hour work week.
* **Temporary Staff and Contractors:** Unless specified in their individual contract, temporary staff and external contractors do not accrue paid annual leave.

## 3. Carry-Over and Forfeiture
* **Maximum Carry-Over:** Employees are encouraged to use all their annual leave within the current calendar year. A maximum of 5 unused leave days may be carried over into the subsequent calendar year.
* **Forfeiture Deadline:** Any carried-over leave must be fully utilized by March 31st of the subsequent calendar year. Any carried-over days remaining after March 31st will be automatically forfeited without financial compensation.
* **No Buy-Back:** Except upon termination of employment, ttb Policy Assistant Demo Bank does not buy back unused annual leave days.
```

## 4. Request and Notice Guidelines
To ensure business continuity, employees must submit leave requests with sufficient notice:
* **Short Leave (1 to 4 days):** Must be requested at least 5 business days in advance.
* **Long Leave (5 or more consecutive days):** Must be requested at least 15 business days in advance.
* **Peak Season Bookings:** Leave requests for peak periods (June 15th to August 31st, and December 15th to January 5th) must be submitted during the annual planning window, which opens on October 1st and closes on November 15th of the prior year.
```

## 5. Approval Authority and Workflows
* **Up to 4 Days:** Leave requests of up to 4 consecutive business days require approval from the employee's immediate Line Manager.
* **5 Days or More:** Leave requests of 5 or more consecutive business days require a double approval process: first from the immediate Line Manager, and subsequently from the Department Head.
* **Rejection & Rescheduling:** If a manager rejects a leave request due to critical operational needs, they must provide a written reason and work with the employee to reschedule the leave within 30 days.

## 6. Interruptions and Emergency Cancellations
* **Sickness during Leave:** If an employee falls ill during their scheduled annual leave, they may request to convert those days into sick leave. This requires a valid medical certificate from a registered healthcare provider covering the entire sickness period, submitted to People Operations within 2 business days of returning to work.
* **Emergency Recall:** In highly exceptional business emergencies, a Department Head may recall an employee from scheduled annual leave. The bank will reimburse all non-refundable travel costs incurred by the employee, subject to submitting receipts to Finance Operations.
