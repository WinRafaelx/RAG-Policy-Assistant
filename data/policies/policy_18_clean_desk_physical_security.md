# Clean Desk and Physical Security Policy

**Policy ID:** TTB-SEC-006  
**Effective Date:** November 15, 2024  
**Owner Department:** Information Security  

## 1. Clean Desk Guidelines
This policy sets the standards for protecting physical bank workspaces, devices, and paper documents containing sensitive information at ttb Policy Assistant Demo Bank.
* **15-Minute Rule:** All paper documents containing Confidential or Restricted information must be locked in a secure desk drawer or filing cabinet when the employee is away from their desk for more than 15 minutes.
* **End of Day:** At the end of the working day, all paper documents must be cleared from desks and locked away. No sensitive paper files may be left unattended overnight.

## 2. Device Physical Security
* **Session Locking:** Laptops and desktops must be locked (Windows key + L) whenever the employee leaves their desk, regardless of duration.
* **Overnight Security:** Laptops left overnight in the office must be physically secured to the desk using a Kensington lock or locked inside a drawer.
* **Remote Security:** Laptops must not be left unattended in vehicles or public spaces.

## 3. Workplace Perimeter Security
* **Anti-Tailgating:** Tailgating (allowing another person to follow you through a secure door without scanning their badge) is strictly prohibited.
* **Mandatory Badge Scanning:** Badge scanning is mandatory at all 4 internal perimeter checkpoints (Lobby, Floor Access, Data Center Lobby, Cash Vault Entry).
* **Reporting:** Any tailgating incident must be reported immediately to the physical security team.

## 4. Badges and Visitor Management
* **Staff Badges:** Staff badges must be worn visibly at all times above the waist when on bank premises.
* **Visitor Escorting:** Visitors must be escorted by an active ttb employee at all times and must wear a red "Visitor" badge.
* **Lost Badges:** Lost or stolen badges must be reported to physical security within 2 hours of discovery to disable access.

## 5. Restricted Access Areas
* **Vault and Server Access:** Entry to restricted physical areas, such as server rooms and cash vaults, requires formal approval.
* **Approving Authorities:** Access requires joint approval from the Facilities Manager and the Head of Information Security.
* **Logs:** All entries to restricted areas are recorded in the security log and kept for 2 years.

## 6. Audit and Non-Compliance
* **Audits:** Physical security audits are conducted bi-weekly by the Physical Security Team.
* **Reporting Cycle:** Audit findings and non-compliance cases are reported to the employee's Line Manager within 24 hours of audit completion. Repeated non-compliance will lead to formal disciplinary review.
