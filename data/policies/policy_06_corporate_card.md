# Corporate Card Policy

**Policy ID:** TTB-FIN-003  
**Effective Date:** February 1, 2025  
**Owner Department:** Finance Operations  

## 1. Eligibility and Issuance
This policy governs the issuance, usage, and management of corporate credit cards provided by ttb Policy Assistant Demo Bank.
* **Eligibility Criteria:** Corporate cards are issued only to permanent employees who meet at least one of the following criteria:
  1. Required to travel on official bank business at least 4 times per calendar year.
  2. Routinely incur monthly business expenses (excluding subscriptions) exceeding $500.00 USD.
* **Application:** Applications require a business case, Line Manager recommendation, and sign-off from the Corporate Card Admin Team.

## 2. Spending Limits and Tiers
Corporate cards are configured with monthly credit limits based on the employee's role and business needs:
* **Standard Tier:** Capped at a monthly spending limit of $5,000.00 USD. This is the default limit for most staff.
* **Executive Tier:** Capped at a monthly spending limit of $15,000.00 USD. This tier is reserved for Managing Directors, Department Heads, and Executive Leadership.
* **Temporary Increases:** Temporary limit increases for specific business trips or projects must be approved by the Department Head and the Head of Finance Operations.

## 3. Reconciliation Timelines and Standards
* **Reconciliation Deadline:** Cardholders are responsible for reconciling all transactions in the corporate expense management system by the 10th business day of the subsequent month.
* **Receipt Matching:** Every transaction must be matched with an itemized receipt (matching the requirements of the Expense Reimbursement Policy).
* **Disputed Transactions:** Disputed charges must be reported to the card issuer and the Card Admin Team within 5 business days of the statement date.

## 4. Prohibited Card Activities
Corporate cards are strictly for business-related transactions. Prohibited activities include:
* **Personal Expenses:** Charging any personal purchases, even if the employee intends to pay the bank back.
* **Cash Advances:** Using the card at an ATM to withdraw cash is strictly prohibited. An exception is permitted only for international travel to remote locations where credit cards are not accepted, which must be pre-authorized in writing by the Card Admin Team.
* **Split Transactions:** Splitting a single purchase into multiple transactions to bypass spending or approval limits is a violation.

## 5. Delinquency and Suspension
* **Late Reconciliation:** If a cardholder has unreconciled transactions older than 45 days, the card will be automatically suspended by the Finance Operations Card Team.
* **Reactivation:** Suspended cards will only be reactivated once all outstanding transactions are reconciled and approved by the employee's Line Manager.
* **Repeated Delinquency:** Employees who have their card suspended 3 times within a 12-month period will have their card permanently revoked.

## 6. Escalations
Suspected card abuse or fraud will be escalated immediately to the Finance Operations Audit Team and the HR Relations Team. Any policy exceptions or appeals regarding card revocation must be submitted to the Head of Finance Operations for final decision.
