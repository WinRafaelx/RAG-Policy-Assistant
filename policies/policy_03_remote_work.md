# Remote Work Policy

**Policy ID:** TTB-HR-003  
**Effective Date:** March 15, 2025  
**Owner Department:** People Operations  

## 1. Objectives and Scope
This policy establishes the rules and frameworks for hybrid and remote work arrangements at ttb Policy Assistant Demo Bank. The policy balances operational efficiency, information security, and employee flexibility, ensuring that customer data and bank systems remain secure regardless of work location.

## 2. Hybrid Work Framework
* **Standard Hybrid Model:** The default working model for eligible roles is hybrid. Under this model, employees are required to work from a physical ttb corporate office at least 3 days per week.
* **Core Office Days:** Tuesday, Wednesday, and Thursday are designated as core office days. All hybrid employees must be physically present on these days to facilitate in-person collaboration, meetings, and team alignment.
* **Flexible Days:** Monday and Friday are flexible days where employees may work from their approved remote location or the office.

## 3. Remote Workspace Requirements
To be eligible to work remotely, employees must ensure their home workspace meets the following standards:
* **Internet Connectivity:** Employees must maintain an active high-speed internet connection. The minimum required speeds are a download speed of 50 Mbps and an upload speed of 10 Mbps. Proof of a speed test must be submitted during the application process.
* **Physical Security:** The remote workspace must be private and secure. Staff must ensure screens are not visible to family members or visitors, and confidential discussions cannot be overheard.
* **Ergonomics:** The workspace must be equipped with a suitable desk, chair, and adequate lighting to prevent health and safety issues.

## 4. Fully Remote Work Exceptions
* **Eligibility:** Fully remote arrangements (0 office days per week) are exceptional. They are restricted to employees whose primary residence is located more than 100 kilometers away from the nearest ttb corporate office.
* **Work Location restriction:** Fully remote employees must work from their registered residential address. Working from public places (e.g., coffee shops, public libraries) or from international locations is strictly prohibited due to data sovereignty and tax compliance rules.

## 5. Approval Process and Workflows
* **Hybrid Arrangement:** Agreed directly between the employee and their Line Manager during the annual review or upon hiring.
* **Fully Remote Request:** Requires a formal application via the HR Portal. The request must be approved by the employee's Line Manager, their Department Head, and the Executive Vice President of People Operations.
* **Review Cycle:** All remote work approvals are subject to annual review and may be revoked with 30 days' written notice if performance drops or business requirements change.

## 6. Core Working Hours and Availability
* **Core Hours:** All employees, whether working hybrid or fully remote, must be available during ttb's core operational hours: 09:00 AM to 05:00 PM local time.
* **Communication responsiveness:** Staff are expected to respond to urgent internal communications (email, phone, or messaging tool) within 60 minutes during core working hours.

## 7. Appeals and Escalations
If an employee's request for hybrid or fully remote work is denied, they may appeal the decision. Appeals must be escalated in writing to the Remote Work Committee, which meets on the first Tuesday of each month to review pending cases.
